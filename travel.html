How to Reach Solapur

By Road

Solapur is connected to major cities by highways.

  • Pune – 250 km
  • Hyderabad – 300 km
  • Mumbai – 400 km
  • Bengaluru – 380 km

By Rail

Solapur Railway Station is a major railway junction connecting cities like Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Bengaluru.

By Air

The nearest airports are Pune International Airport and Hyderabad International Airport.

Local Transport

  • Auto Rickshaws
  • Taxis
  • City Buses
