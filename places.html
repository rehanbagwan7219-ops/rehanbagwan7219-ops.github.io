Siddheshwar Temple

Siddheshwar Temple is one of the most important spiritual landmarks in Solapur. Dedicated to Lord Siddheshwar, a form of Lord Shiva, the temple is located near Siddheshwar Lake in the center of the city.

Thousands of devotees visit the temple every day and especially during the Siddheshwar Yatra festival held every year in January.

Pandharpur

Pandharpur is one of the most sacred pilgrimage towns in Maharashtra located about 70 km from Solapur. It is famous for the Vitthal Rukmini Temple.

Akkalkot

Akkalkot is located about 40 km from Solapur and is famous for the temple of Shri Swami Samarth Maharaj. Devotees believe Swami Samarth blesses people and helps them overcome difficulties.

Solapur Fort

Solapur Fort also called Bhuikot Fort is a historic monument built during the Bahmani Sultanate period and represents the rich heritage of the city.
