Pandharpur Pandharpur is one of the most important pilgrimage cities in Maharashtra. It is famous for the Vitthal-Rukmini temple and attracts lakhs of devotees during Ashadhi and Kartiki Ekadashi. Pandharpur is around 75 km from Solapur. You can reach by:

• Bus from Solapur Bus Stand
• Train to Pandharpur Railway Station
• Private vehicles via Solapur–Pandharpur highway
Siddheshwar Temple Siddheshwar Temple is a famous temple located in Solapur city. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva and situated in the middle of a beautiful lake. Located in Solapur city center.

• Easily reachable by auto or bus
• 10 minutes from Solapur Railway Station
• Walkable from Siddheshwar Lake area
Tuljapur Tuljapur is famous for the Tulja Bhavani Temple, one of the 51 Shakti Peethas in India. It is an important religious place visited by devotees from all over the country. Tuljapur is about 45 km from Solapur.

• Frequent buses from Solapur Bus Stand
• Well connected by road via NH-52
• Nearest railway station: Solapur
