Siddheshwar Temple

Siddheshwar Temple is one of the most important spiritual landmarks in Solapur. Dedicated to Lord Siddheshwar, a form of Lord Shiva.

Pandharpur

Pandharpur is one of the most sacred pilgrimage towns in Maharashtra located about 70 km from Solapur.

Tuljapur

Tuljapur is famous for the Tulja Bhavani Temple which attracts devotees from across Maharashtra.

Solapur Fort

Solapur Fort also called Bhuikot Fort is a historic monument built during the Bahmani Sultanate period.
